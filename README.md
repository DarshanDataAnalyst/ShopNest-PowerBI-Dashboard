## 📌 Project Overview

This project was developed as a capstone submission to demonstrate end-to-end Business Intelligence capabilities using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard answers 8 critical business questions across sales, logistics, customer behavior, and revenue performance — enabling data-driven decisions for an e-commerce business.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Modules & Business Questions Solved

| # | Dashboard Module | Business Question |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|
| 1 | 🏆 **Top 10 Product Categories** | Which product categories generate the highest total sales? |
| 2 | 🚚 **Delayed Orders Analysis** | What is the proportion of delayed vs. on-time deliveries? |
| 3 | 📈 **Sales Trend Over Time** | How do sales fluctuate across quarters and years? |
| 4 | 💳 **Payment Method Analysis** | What payment methods do customers prefer? |
| 5 | ⭐ **Product Rating Analysis** | Which are the top 10 & bottom 10 rated products? |
| 6 | 🗺️ **State-wise Sales Analysis** | Which states have the highest and lowest sales performance? |
| 7 | 🌤️ **Seasonal Sales Patterns** | Which quarter sees maximum orders across years? |
| 8 | 💰 **Revenue Analysis** | How has total revenue changed over time for ShopNest? |

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. 🏆 Top 10 Product Categories by Total Sales
- Identifies the highest-revenue generating product categories.
- Enables targeted **inventory management** and **marketing investment** decisions.
- Helps leadership focus resources on categories with the most revenue impact.

### 2. 🚚 Delayed Orders Analysis
- Visualizes the split between **on-time** and **delayed** deliveries.
- Root cause: Actual delivery time exceeding the scheduled delivery window.
- **Recommendation:** Increasing delivery workforce during peak periods can reduce delays and improve customer satisfaction.
- Color coding: 🔴 Red = On-time | 🔵 Blue = Delayed | 🌑 Dark Blue = No orders

### 3. 📈 Sales Trend Over Time
- Column chart reveals **peak seasons** and **long-term growth patterns**.
- Quarterly and yearly distribution helps identify periods of revenue growth and decline.
- Supports forecasting and seasonal staffing decisions.

### 4. 💳 Payment Method Analysis
- Breaks down customer payment preferences:
  - Credit Card
  - Boleto
  - Voucher
  - Debit Card
  - Cash
- Enables the business to optimize payment gateway investments.

### 5. ⭐ Product Rating Analysis
- **Top 10 Rated Products:** Best-reviewed items with highest customer satisfaction scores.
- **Bottom 10 Rated Products:** Low-rated items flagging quality/service issues.
- Useful for product improvement strategies and supplier negotiations.

### 6. 🗺️ State-wise Sales Analysis
- Shows total sales, total orders delivered, and average ratings **per state**.
- Identifies geographic revenue concentration.
- Useful for regional logistics planning and territory-based strategies.

### 7. 🌤️ Seasonal Sales Pattern
- Quarterly analysis across multiple years reveals which quarter sees peak orders.
- **Actionable:** Deploy more delivery staff in high-demand quarters to protect revenue and reduce delays.

### 8. 💰 Revenue Analysis
- Tracks total revenue generated by ShopNest Store across years.
- Highlights drawback periods and growth opportunities for strategic planning.

## 🚀 How to Open & Run the Dashboard

### Prerequisites
- Install **[Microsoft Power BI Desktop](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/desktop/)** (Free)

### Steps
1. **Clone this repository:**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/DarshanDataAnalyst/ShopNest-PowerBI-Dashboard.git
   ```

2. **Navigate to the project folder:**
   ```bash
   cd ShopNest-PowerBI-Dashboard
   ```

3. **Open the dashboard:**
   - Double-click `power_bi_assignment.pbix`, OR
   - Open Power BI Desktop → File → Open → select `power_bi_assignment.pbix`

4. **Explore the dashboards:**
   - Navigate through each report tab at the bottom.
   - Use slicers/filters to interact with visuals dynamically.
